Pentagon Actions. Two thousand women met on the Pentagon to demand:

1990

Karen J. Warren

The history of ecofeminism

1990

In an article entitled "The Power and Promise of Ecological Feminism », Karen J. Warren highlights the growth of ecofeminism, with both :

  • an importance of feminism to environmental ethics.
  • the importance of environmentalism for feminism.
  • Research, workshops and articles on the subject are multiplying, testifying to the vitality of this movement.

2019
